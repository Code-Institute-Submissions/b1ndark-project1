Beaches

Camilo Beach


Located near the city of Lagos is the Camilo beach, covering a moderate area of sand, and well known for its beautiful sunrise.

The beach is located in between cliffs and is reached via a stairway involving around 200 steps.


Average seawater temperature in summer(ºC): 20-22 ºC


GPS location: Camilo Beach

Carvalho Beach


Located near Benagil is the Carvalho beach, the beach is completely surrounded by rocks, if it wasn’t for its 15 meter long hand-carved tunnel it would only have been possible to access it by the sea.

Well worth the trip to enjoy this breath taking beach and views.


Average seawater temperature in summer(ºC): 20ºC


GPS location: Carvalho Beach

Rocha Beach


Located in the city of Portimao, the Rocha beach is well known for its dimensions, easy access and the infrastructures that offers such as restaurants and bars, it definitely attracts young families to it, so they can enjoy a lovely sunny day, and it also offers plenty of water sport activities.


Average seawater temperature in summer(ºC): 20-23 ºC


GPS location: Rocha Beach
