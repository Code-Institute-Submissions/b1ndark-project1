Beaches

Camilo Beach


Located near the city of Lagos is the Camilo beach, covering a moderate area of sand, and well known for its beautiful sunrise.

The beach is located in between cliffs and is reached via a stairway involving around 200 steps.


Average seawater temperature in summer(ºC): 20-22 ºC
