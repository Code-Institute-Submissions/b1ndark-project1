Activities

Cave Tours


Exploring the caves of such a beautiful coastline is definitely an unforgettable experience where you can also encounter wild dolphins to contemplate the experience of the moment.

The top attraction of the cave tours is definitely the beach inside the cave of Benagil.


There are plenty of agencies offering cave tours along Algarve beautiful coastline.


GPS location: Benagil Cave








Carvalho Beach


Located near Benagil is the Carvalho beach, the beach is completely surrounded by rocks, if it wasn’t for its 15 metre long hand-carved tunnel it would only have been possible to access it by sea.

Well worth the trip to enjoy this breath taking beach and views.


Average seawater temperature in summer(ºC): 20ºC


GPS location: Carvalho Beach








Rocha Beach


Located in the city of Portimao, the beach is well known for its dimensions, easy to access and the infractures that offers such as restaurants and bars it definitely attracts young families to it to enjoy a lovely sunny day, and it also offers plenty of water sport activities.


Average seawater temperature in summer(ºC): 20-23 ºC


GPS location: Rocha Beach
