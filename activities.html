Activities

Cave Tours


Exploring the caves of such a beautiful coastline is definitely an unforgettable experience where you can also encounter wild dolphins to contemplate the experience of the moment.

The top attraction of the cave tours is definitely the beach inside of the Benagil cave.


There are plenty of agencies offering cave tours along Algarve beautiful coastline.


GPS location: Benagil Cave

Skydiving


It definitely offers an unforgettable experience of a life time, thrill and experience of freefalling from 15000ft(4600m) above sea level.

A chance to see the beauty of Algarve coastline and surroundings from above.


There are many agencies that offer this experience of a life time.


GPS location: Skydive Algarve

Zoomarine


Zoomarine offers a diversity of experiences for all types families, from programmes where you can experience live moments of tenderness and affection with Dolphins, to the amusements offered to explore in the park.


Zoomarine also has a beach which is a big aquatic fun area, where you can explore the offered slides.


GPS location: Zoomarine
